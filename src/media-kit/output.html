About Us

Established in 1969, The Badger Herald is one of the first independent and student run newspapers in America. Since its establishment, it has been awarded one of the top five independent newspapers in the country along with countless other awards.

Due to the physical lack of students on campus, we have decided to suspend our print issue for the rest of the semester and until in-person classes resume. In its place, we are publishing two newsletters a week to send the latest articles straight to email inboxes.
